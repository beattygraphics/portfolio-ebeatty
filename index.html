About Me

Hello, and Welcome to my Page! My name is Erin Beatty and I am currently the Director of IT and Marketing for Gas Express LLC. We are the Largest Circle K Franchise group in the nation. We currently operate 106 stores in 5 states, and expect to be 200 Stores by the end of 2022. I have been working in the IT field for 10 years, mostly focused in the Convenience and Fueling sector. Before I branched into IT, I was a Graphic Production Artist. I mostly focused on Trade Show Graphics, Large Format Digital Imaging, Vehicle/Building Wraps, and Mixed Media. When I'm not working (which isnt often), I like to travel. Escpecially when the destination is a beach. If I had a Hobby, it would be playing Texas Hold'em Poker. I hope you like what you see!
