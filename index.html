About Me

Hello, and Welcome to my Page! My name is Erin Beatty and I am currently the Director of IT and Marketing for Gas Express LLC. We are the Largest Circle K Franchise group in the nation. We currently operate 106 Circle K and Kangaroo Express stores in 5 states throughout the South East. We also have 3 Major QSR's (Food Service offerings) in Krispy Krunchy Chicken, Subway and Champs Chicken. Our Company goal is to grow to 200 Stores by the end of 2022. I have worked in the IT field for a little over 10 years, mostly focused in the Convenience and Fueling sector. Before working in the IT sector, I was a Graphic Designer and Graphics Production Artist. My design and production focus was Trade Show Graphics, Large Format Digital Imaging, Vehicle/Building Wraps, and Mixed Media. When I'm not working (which isnt often), I like to travel and play in my garden. I currently have 5 different variets of Peppers that i cultivate year round. The perfect travel destinatioon for me is a beach. If I had a Hobby, it would be playing Texas Hold'em Poker. I love to play cards. It is fun to read people and to keep my brain young. Thank you again for visiting my site. I hope you like what you see!
